Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my oil changed?

For most modern vehicles, we recommend changing your oil every 5,000 to 7,500 miles. However, this can vary based on your driving habits, vehicle age, and the type of oil used. Check your owner's manual for manufacturer recommendations, or consult with our technicians for personalized advice.

What's included in a multi-point inspection?

Our multi-point inspection covers over 30 key components of your vehicle, including brakes, tires, suspension, steering, exhaust system, belts, hoses, fluids, lights, and filters. This comprehensive check helps identify potential issues before they become major problems, saving you time and money in the long run.

How long does a typical service appointment take?

Most basic services like oil changes take about 30-45 minutes. More comprehensive services like brake work or tire rotations typically take 1-2 hours. For major services or repairs, we'll provide you with a time estimate when you schedule your appointment. We also offer shuttle service and loaner vehicles for longer repairs.

Do you use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts?

We offer both OEM parts and high-quality aftermarket parts. Our technicians will discuss the options with you and recommend the best choice for your vehicle and budget. All parts we install come with a warranty for your peace of mind.
