Frequently Asked Questions

What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday from 9am to 8pm EST, and on Saturdays from 10am to 6pm EST. For emergency roadside assistance, we're available 24/7.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line directly.

Do you offer support in languages other than English?

Yes, we have support representatives who speak Spanish and French. Please let us know your language preference when you contact us.

What information should I have ready when contacting support?

For faster service, please have your account number, vehicle information, and any relevant documentation ready when you contact our support team.
