Frequently Asked Questions

What is covered under an extended warranty?

Coverage varies by plan, but typically includes major components like engine, transmission, drivetrain, electrical systems, and more. Our bumper-to-bumper coverage offers the most comprehensive protection, while powertrain plans focus on essential components.

Can I use my own mechanic?

Yes, you can choose any licensed repair facility. However, for the claims process to be most efficient, we recommend using one of our certified repair facilities within our nationwide network.

How long does the coverage last?

Coverage terms vary based on the plan you select. We offer options ranging from 2 to 7 years of additional coverage beyond the manufacturer's warranty, or specific mileage limits up to 150,000 miles.

Are there any deductibles?

Yes, most plans have a deductible that you pay per repair visit. We offer deductible options ranging from $0 to $200, allowing you to choose a plan that fits your budget and needs.
